## 🔴 P0 — fix immediately

**Booking slots are 1 hour wrong during British Summer Time** — `BLD-329`,
`lib/availability.ts`. Slot wall-clock was computed in server-local UTC
(`new Date(dateISO+'T00:00:00')`, `setHours`/`getHours`) while opening hours are
Europe/London. During BST (~7 months/year, including now) an intended 09:00 slot
was offered at 10:00 London — the 09:00 hour lost, a slot an hour past close
offered. **✅ FIXED this PR** — `lib/clinic-time.ts` gained proven
London↔UTC helpers; all slot math routes through them; verified for BST + GMT.

## 🟠 P1 — high value

- **Portal multi-item booking still races** — `BLD-317`,
  `app/api/booking/start/route.ts:155`. The public `create` path got the
  Serializable transaction; this authenticated path still does a bare
  `booking.create` with no overlap re-check. Port the same pattern.
- **Reminder send-failure burns the window flag** — `BLD-331`. **✅ FIXED.**
- **Academy portal has no session revocation, no password reset, no erasure**
  — `BLD-314` (epic, see `docs/projects/academy-portal-parity.md`). The academy
  was built fast and never inherited the client-portal epoch/reset/erasure
  machinery; a suspended trainee keeps a 7-day token and cannot be erased.
- **Accessibility AA failures** — `BLD-313` (epic, see
  `docs/projects/accessibility-aa.md`). Brand-gold text fails contrast
  (2.75:1) on conversion-critical copy; several forms have unassociated
  `<label>`s; overlay modals lack focus management.
- **Shop has no Product schema + is absent from the sitemap** — `BLD-` (SEO).
  Revenue pages invisible to discovery, no product rich-results.
